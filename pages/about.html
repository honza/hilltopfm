We are a non-profit, commercial free Christian station that provides music and spoken-word shows to the HRM community 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

We are staffed entirely by volunteers and broadcast out of Bedford Baptist Church perched on the top of a hill (thus the name) overlooking the community of Bedford, Nova Scotia. Our purpose is to provide our listening audience with a message of hope for their lives through the songs and messages we broadcast on our station.
